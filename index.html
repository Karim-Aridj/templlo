Frequently Asked Questions

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. We can sometimes arrange for early check-in or late check-out for a small fee, subject to availability.

Yes, all our rooms and apartments are equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, perfect for remote work or streaming.

Policy for Cancellations will be charged 100% of total room nights at confirmed rate. With the exception of authorized cancellations, we require 30 days cancellation notice prior to your scheduled arrival date for a 100% refund, Otherwise a penalty of 100% of full reservation will apply.

You cannot visit the property in person. However, you can request a video call for a virtual tour of the property you are interested in.

Reservations can be made by anyone older than 18 years old. We have strict house rules to ensure clean and quiet stays.

To access our apartments, we use security boxes at both the building and apartment entrances. This means you will be provided with codes before your arrival. The keys for the building and apartment are shared among guests, while your room key is for your private use only.

Rooms are available from November 1st, 2025, until February 28th, 2026. We prefer monthly short-term rentals, but shorter stays may be available upon request.
